# ADR-007 License Compliance Process

- Status: accepted
- First needed: end of P3

## Context

Redistribution needed a small, deterministic release-time compliance process
that kept required artifacts and dependency references explicit.

## Decision

Releases must ship root `LICENSE` and `NOTICE`, keep vendored Ghidra
attribution explicit through `NOTICE` plus the `third_party/ghidra` submodule
pointer and pass `python tools/compliance.py` before tagging.
The compliance, footprint,
release-readiness, and artifact-audit helpers remain repo-only tooling and stay
out of wheel and sdist payloads.

## Consequences

- Redistribution requirements remain reviewable through committed notice/artifact
  files and deterministic audit tooling.
- Compliance policy stays separate from the default-install footprint policy.
- Build and release helpers remain maintainer tooling, not part of the shipped
  package surface.
